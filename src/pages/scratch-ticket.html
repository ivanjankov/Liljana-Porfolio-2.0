Scratch Ticket

CLIENT: National Lottery of N. Macedonia.

This project is all about celebrating birthdays in a new and exciting way! Each ticket has a special meaning, making them unique and memorable. The idea is to congratulate someone without even saying a word. It's a fresh and fun way to say 'Happy Birthday' that's sure to bring a smile to anyone's face.

These tickets are a mystery in themselves, holding endless possibilities and excitement. They're a unique way to make someone's birthday special, and who knows what kind of surprises they might hold?
