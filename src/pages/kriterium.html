Kriterium Music

CLIENT: Piano Gallery - Kriterium Music, N.Macedonia

Kriterium Music is a luxury piano gallery that truly embodies the artistry and sophistication of the piano. As one of my favorite clients, I've had the pleasure of working on their branding, and it's been an exciting journey to capture the essence of their gallery through design

One of the most fun aspects of branding Kriterium Music is creating eye-catching posters and flyers that really capture the attention of potential customers. These designs have been incredibly effective at drawing people in and piquing their interest in the gallery

The goal of branding Kriterium Music is to create a luxurious and memorable experience for customers. I strive to capture the elegance and sophistication of the pianos, and to showcase the beauty and artistry that goes into each instrument. By working closely with the team at Kriterium Music, we've been able to create a visual identity that truly embodies the essence of the gallery, and that draws people in to explore the world of luxury pianos.
